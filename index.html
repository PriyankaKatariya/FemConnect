FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This website is designed to provide a supportive and inclusive online community for women in the tech industry. It offers resources, mentorship opportunities, and networking events to help women advance their careers and achieve their goals.

Who is this website for?

This website is open to all women who are interested in technology, regardless of their current level of experience or education. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, you're welcome to join our community.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get involved in our community. You can join our online forums, participate in our events, connect with mentors or mentees, or contribute to our blog.

Is there a cost to join the community?

Many of our resources and events are free of charge. However, some may require a small fee to cover costs. We strive to make our community accessible to everyone, and we offer scholarships and payment plans to help those who may need financial assistance.
